Guide for Author

Authors must declare that the article is not published (part or whole except in the form of an abstract, a published lecture or academic thesis) elsewhere. Each author is required to declare his or her individual contribution to the research and/or article preparation.The statement that all authors have approved the final article should be true and included in the disclosure. Authors are expected to consider carefully the list and order of authors before submitting their manuscript and provide the definitive list of authors at the time of the original submission. Any addition, deletion or rearrangement of author names in the authorship list should be made after approvalof the journal Editor. For changing corresponding authorship, the Editor must receive a request mentioning the reason for the change in author list and a written confirmation (e-mail/letter) from all authors that they agree with the addition, removal or rearrangement. Authors must declare to follow the copywrite policy of the journal. Permission of the Publisher is required for resale, translations or compilations of published article.

Manuscript preparation

The manuscript should be in the following sequence: abstract and keywords, introduction, materials and methods, results, discussion, acknowledgments, conflict of interest, references. All materials must be written in proper and clear English.

Title: Style Book Antiqua, Bold, 12 point font
Author/s: Book Antiqua , normal 10 point font (Maximum 8 name)
Affiliation: Book Antiqua , normal 10 ppt
Corresponding author: e-mail:-
Abstract: Style Book Antiqua , normal 10 point font (Maximum 250 words)
Key words: Maximum 6

Introduction, Materials and methods, Results and Discussion: The manuscript including tables and their footnotes, and figure legends, must be typed out in Double space, standard 11 point font in Book Antiqua style (For final accepted manuscript single space, standard 11 point font, Book Antiqua style).

References: The number of references should be less than forty (40) for original article and fifteen (15) for short communication and case report. If possible DOI should be added with the references. Follow the styles shown in the example below:

Reference to a journal article: Powell DW, Mifflin RC, Valentich JD, Crowe SE, Saada JI, West AB, 1999. Myofibroblasts. II. Intestinal subepithelial myofibroblasts. The American Journal of Physiology 277: C183-201. doi:10.1016/j.heliyon.2018.e00205

Reference to a conference paper: Powell DW, Mifflin RC, Valentich JD, Crowe SE, Saada JI, West AB, 1999Name of conference, page number of proceeding abstract.

Reference to a book: Mettam GR, Adams LB. How to prepare an electronic version of your article. In: Jones BS, Smith RZ, editors. Introduction to the electronic age, New York: E-Publishing Inc; 2009, p. 281–304.

Reference to a website: Cancer Research UK. Cancer statistics reports for the UK, http://www.cancerresearchuk.org/ aboutcancer/statistics/cancerstatsreport/; 2003

Publication charge: The BVJ will charge the publication cost to manuscript submitted as bellows.
From Bangladeshi corresponding authors: 2500 BD TK
From foreign corresponding authors: ＄32

If there is any query concerning manuscript submission, contact by e-mail to the Editor of BVJ.
