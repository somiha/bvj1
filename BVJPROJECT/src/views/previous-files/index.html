Bangladesh Veterinary Journal

Bangladesh Veterinary Journal(BVJ) is a peer-reviewed journal encompasses all aspects of veterinary research and medicine of domestics, wild and lab animals and birds including aquatic medicine. With the glorious and revolutionary birth of Bangladesh, the Bangladesh veterinary Journal is also born. It is the official journal published by Bangladesh Veterinary Association (BVA), but welcomes submissions from veterinary researchers worldwide. BVJ publishes research articles, review articles and clinical cases or case studies that has never been published and is not currently being considered for publication elsewhere.BVJ is dedicated to bring into publication original research articles, review articles, case reports, Brief communications and letter to editor from veterinary scientists of our country and abroad.

Editor: Professor Nasrin Sultana Juyena, PhD

SCOPES:
BVJ publishes articles on a wide range of topics of basic and applied researches in

  • Food safety and public health
  • Zoonosis, one health and AMR
  • Epidemiology and risk analysis
  • Veterinary Bio-medical Sciences (Anatomy, Histology, Embryology, genetic and breeding, physiology and immunology
  • Veterinary Pathobiology (Microbiology, Parasitology, Pathology)
  • Nutrition and Metabolic diseases
  • Food animal Veterinary practices (Medicine, Surgery and Theriogenology)
  • Avian medicine and biosecurity
  • Economy of Animal Health
  • Animal Health Communication (extension, education and community service)

Peer-review policy

BVJ operates a double-blind peer-review system, where the reviewers and the authors are not aware of the names and affiliation of each other. Submissions are initially assessed by the editor. Manuscripts will then be reviewed by two experts who will be asked to evaluate whether the manuscript is scientifically sound for publication.
